The Offspring Announce Full Line-Up for Crystal Palace Park Show

Dropkick Murphys, Pennywise, PUP, and Destroy Boys join the California punk veterans for a landmark outdoor event.

The Bulletin — June 24, 2026
Photo: Wikimedia Commons / Wikimedia (CC0)

The Offspring have confirmed the full stage times and supporting line-up for their upcoming headline show at Crystal Palace Park in London.

Joining the band as special guests are Dropkick Murphys, Pennywise, PUP, and Destroy Boys — a bill that brings together multiple generations of punk and punk-adjacent acts under a single outdoor stage. The Dropkick Murphys, formed in Boston in 1996, have long operated in the Celtic punk space and have maintained an active touring presence for nearly three decades. Pennywise, formed in Hermosa Beach, California in 1988, share geographic and stylistic roots with The Offspring, both emerging from the Southern California punk scene that produced a wave of commercially successful hard punk acts in the 1990s. PUP, the Toronto-based punk quartet, have built a significant following since their 2013 formation, while Sacramento's Destroy Boys represent a newer strain of the genre.

The Offspring, formed in Garden Grove, California in 1984, rose to mainstream visibility with their 1994 album Smash, which became one of the best-selling independent releases in rock history at the time of its release. Crystal Palace Park has served as a major outdoor concert venue in South London for decades, hosting large-scale events across multiple genres.

Full stage times for the event are now available via NME, which first reported the confirmed schedule.
