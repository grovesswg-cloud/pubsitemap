Turnstile Announce 2026 North American Tour With Eclectic Opener Lineup

The Baltimore hardcore band continues its practice of assembling cross-genre bills, this time pairing with Slayyyter, Clipse, and Vince Staples.

The Bulletin — June 24, 2026
Photo: Wikipedia — Turnstile (band) / Wikipedia

Turnstile have announced a 2026 North American tour featuring support slots from Slayyyter, Clipse, Vince Staples, and additional acts yet to be detailed.

The announcement continues a pattern the band established on their 2025 North American run, which brought together Blood Orange, Mannequin Pussy, Amyl and the Sniffers, Speed, and Jane Remover on a single touring cycle. The 2025 bill drew documented attention for its range across hardcore, indie rock, punk, and electronic music.

The 2026 lineup extends that range further. Clipse, the Virginia rap duo of Pusha T and No Malice, released their first album in sixteen years with Let God Sort Em Out in 2024. Vince Staples is a Long Beach rapper whose catalog spans XL Recordings releases dating back to 2015. Slayyyter is a hyperpop and dance-pop artist from Nashville whose second album, Starfucker, was released in 2023.

Turnstile's 2022 album GLOW ON, released on Roadrunner Records, brought the band mainstream visibility beyond the hardcore circuit and was cited in year-end lists across publications covering rock, pop, and hip-hop. The band's live reputation has been documented extensively in that period, with their performances noted for high-energy production and broad audience crossover.

The full list of supporting acts, specific dates, and venues for the 2026 tour have been announced via the band's official channels, as reported by Stereogum.
